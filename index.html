Purchase Info

All posters come secured in a cardboard tube.

Delivery is currently only supplied to the UK. If you wish to purchase and live outside of the UK, please get in touch.

Delivery can take up to 14 days - please be patient!



For the Geeks

This service uses JavaScript and PHP to generate screenshots of YouTube videos at various timeframes.

The video length is used to base how often a screenshot is required, to fit the 24 spaces in the poster.

The frontend uses SASS, BEM and jQuery.

If you wish to donate, or contribute to this service, please get in touch! It would be awesome to get some help!
